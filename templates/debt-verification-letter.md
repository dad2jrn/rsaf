# Template — Debt Verification / Proof of Assignment Request

> This template must be adapted to the governing statute, timing, and recipient. FDCPA validation rights may not apply to every plaintiff or every procedural posture.

```text
[DATE]

[COLLECTOR / PLAINTIFF / COUNSEL]
[ADDRESS]

Re: Request for Validation and Proof of Present Right to Enforce
Account: [ACCOUNT IDENTIFIER]
Case No.: [CASE NUMBER, if applicable]

To Whom It May Concern:

I dispute the alleged debt and request verification of the debt and proof of your present legal right to collect or enforce it.

Please provide all documents establishing your authority to collect this specific receivable, including but not limited to:

1. The cardmember agreement allegedly governing the account.
2. The complete chain of title for the receivable.
3. Any bill of sale, assignment, reassignment, or transfer agreement identifying the account.
4. Any schedule, exhibit, or account-level record linking the alleged receivable to me.
5. Any document showing that the receivable was never transferred, if that is your position.
6. Any document showing that the receivable was transferred and later reacquired, if that is your position.
7. The name of the current creditor or owner of the receivable.

This request is not an admission of liability. All rights are reserved.

Sincerely,

[NAME]
```
