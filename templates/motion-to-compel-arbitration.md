# Template — Motion to Compel Arbitration

> Use only where an arbitration agreement exists and applies. Verify waiver, forum rules, consumer fee provisions, and court-specific procedure.

## Required Inputs

- Court.
- Case number.
- Parties.
- Arbitration clause.
- Cardmember agreement.
- Governing law.
- Prior litigation conduct.
- Requested stay or dismissal.

## Core Argument Structure

1. The parties entered an agreement containing an arbitration provision.
2. Plaintiff's claim falls within the scope of that provision.
3. Defendant has not waived arbitration.
4. The court should compel arbitration and stay or dismiss the litigation as permitted by law.
