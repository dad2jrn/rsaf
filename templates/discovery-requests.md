# Template — Discovery Requests Focused on Receivable Ownership

## Requests for Production

1. Produce all documents establishing Plaintiff's present ownership of the receivable sued upon.
2. Produce all assignments, bills of sale, reassignments, and schedules identifying Defendant's account.
3. Produce all documents showing that the receivable was never sold, transferred, assigned, securitized, removed, or otherwise conveyed to another entity.
4. Produce all documents showing that the receivable was transferred and later reacquired by Plaintiff.
5. Produce the governing cardmember agreement.
6. Produce any RPA, TSA, PSA, trust agreement, or related agreement Plaintiff contends governs or does not govern the receivable.
7. Produce all business records Plaintiff contends establish the amount claimed.

## Interrogatories

1. Identify every entity that has owned, serviced, transferred, received, or held an interest in the receivable.
2. State whether Plaintiff contends the receivable was ever transferred into a securitization trust.
3. If Plaintiff contends the receivable was never transferred, identify all documents supporting that contention.
4. If Plaintiff contends the receivable was transferred and reacquired, identify each transfer and reacquisition document.

## Requests for Admission

1. Admit that monthly statements do not, by themselves, constitute an executed assignment of the receivable.
2. Admit that Plaintiff bears the burden of proving its present right to enforce the receivable.
3. Admit that Plaintiff has not produced an executed reassignment identifying Defendant's account. [Use only if accurate.]
