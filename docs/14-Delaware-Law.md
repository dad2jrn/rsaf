# 14 — Delaware Law

## 14.1 Scope

This chapter covers Delaware law where the cardmember agreement or governing securitization documents select Delaware law.

## 14.2 Key Topics

- Delaware UCC Article 9.
- Sales of accounts, payment intangibles, chattel paper, and promissory notes.
- Account debtor's right to reasonable proof of assignment.
- Delaware Asset-Backed Securities Facilitation Act.
- Contract interpretation under Delaware law.

## 14.3 Key Statutes to Verify

- 6 Del. C. § 9-109.
- 6 Del. C. § 9-406.
- Delaware Asset-Backed Securities Facilitation Act provisions.

## 14.4 Research Rule

Verify each statute and case before citation. Delaware law may govern contractual rights while Virginia law governs procedure and evidence in a Virginia court.
