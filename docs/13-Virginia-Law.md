# 13 — Virginia Law

## 13.1 Scope

This chapter is a research target for Virginia law relevant to consumer credit-card receivable litigation.

## 13.2 Key Topics

- Standing in Virginia courts.
- Burden of proof in debt collection actions.
- Business records and authentication.
- Account statements as evidence.
- Chain of title for assigned debt.
- Virginia UCC provisions relevant to sales of accounts and payment intangibles.
- Virginia procedural requirements for General District Court and Circuit Court.

## 13.3 Key Authority to Verify

- Green v. Portfolio Recovery Associates, Virginia Court of Appeals.
- Va. Code § 8.9A-109.
- Va. Code § 8.9A-406.
- Virginia rules governing business records and hearsay.

## 13.4 Research Rule

Do not cite this chapter as authority. Cite the underlying verified Virginia statutes, rules, and cases.
