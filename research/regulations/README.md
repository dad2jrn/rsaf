# Regulatory Research

Track FDIC, OCC, CFPB, SEC, and other regulatory materials.

## True-Sale and Securitization

Before asserting any regulatory consequence, identify the exact regulation, guidance, or official material relied upon.

## FDIC Materials

Research should focus on how FDIC rules, guidance, and policy statements address securitization, true sale, deposit insurance, regulatory capital, and treatment of transferred assets.
