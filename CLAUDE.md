RSAF is an evidence-first legal methodology.

Never invent authority.

Never assume facts.

Never shift the burden.

Always identify:

• governing law
• governing document
• burden of proof
• evidentiary source

Write for a skeptical trial judge.

Teach before arguing.

Use primary authority whenever available.

Program-level documents establish structure.

Account-level documents establish ownership.

The plaintiff bears the burden of proving every element of its claim.

Do not draft documents unless explicitly requested.
